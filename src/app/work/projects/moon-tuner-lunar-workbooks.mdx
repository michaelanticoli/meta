## Overview

Moon Tuner is a series of personalized lunar workbooks developed in collaboration with Ang Bocca between 2015-2019. Each workbook is customized to an individual's birth chart, providing month-by-month guidance for working with lunar cycles through journaling prompts, ritual suggestions, and reflective practices.

## Key Features

- **Birth Chart Integration**: Each workbook is personalized based on the user's natal moon sign, house placement, and aspects
- **Monthly Lunar Tracking**: Visual calendars showing new moons, full moons, and quarter phases with corresponding energetic themes
- **Reflection + Ritual Spreads**: Two-page layouts pairing introspective prompts with practical ritual actions
- **Custom Glyphs and Iconography**: Original visual system representing lunar phases, astrological symbols, and elemental correspondences

## Design Approach

The workbooks balance accessibility with depth, offering both beginners and experienced practitioners meaningful entry points.  The layout emphasizes whitespace and clarity, with hand-drawn elements that create an intimate, personal feeling.

## Technologies Used

- **InDesign**: Layout and typography
- **Illustrator**: Custom iconography and glyphs
- **Astrological Software**: Chart calculation and lunar phase tracking
- **Print Production**: High-quality binding and paper selection for tactile experience

## Impact

Moon Tuner workbooks have been used by hundreds of individuals seeking to deepen their relationship with lunar rhythms and their own emotional patterns. The project demonstrated the value of personalized, chart-specific tools in making astrology more practical and actionable for daily life.

## Outcome

The project established a methodology for translating complex astrological data into accessible, beautiful, and functionally useful printed materials. It continues to inform Michael's approach to designing tools that bridge symbolic systems and personal practice.
