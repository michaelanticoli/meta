## Overview

This collection represents Michael's strategic and communication design work—the pitch decks, process diagrams, and frameworks that translate complex ideas into clear, compelling narratives for clients, stakeholders, and collaborators.

## Types of Deliverables

- **Pitch Decks**:  Persuasive presentations that articulate problem, solution, methodology, and value proposition
- **Strategic Frameworks**: Visual systems for organizing thinking and guiding decision-making
- **Process Diagrams**: Clear visualizations of workflows, systems, and methodologies
- **One-Pagers**: Concise overviews of services, products, or project summaries
- **Brand Strategy Documents**: Positioning, messaging architecture, and narrative guidelines

## Core Capabilities

- **Problem/Solution Framing**: Clearly articulating challenges and proposed solutions
- **Visual Storytelling**: Using design and typography to guide audiences through complex ideas
- **Systems Thinking**: Breaking down intricate processes into understandable frameworks
- **Brand Voice**:  Crafting messaging that balances professionalism with personality

## Technologies Used

- **Presentation Design**: Keynote, Google Slides, PowerPoint
- **Visual Design**: Figma, Illustrator, Canva
- **Diagramming**: Miro, Whimsical, Lucidchart
- **Document Design**: InDesign, Google Docs

## Approach

Michael's strategy work is grounded in clarity and empathy.  Every deck begins with understanding the audience:  What do they need to know? What will make them act? The structure follows narrative principles—setup, conflict, resolution—even in business contexts.

## Philosophy

Good strategy work doesn't just inform—it persuades, clarifies, and inspires action. Michael approaches every deck as an opportunity to tell a story that matters.

## Outcome

These deliverables have helped secure funding, win client projects, align teams, and communicate vision across diverse audiences.
