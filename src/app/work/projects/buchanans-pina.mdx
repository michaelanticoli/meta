## Overview

As Lead Strategist for Diageo, I spearheaded the multicultural marketing launch of Buchanan's Piña, a new product targeting diverse Hispanic and Latino audiences. The campaign focused on developing bilingual visual assets and strategic influencer partnerships that authentically connected with multicultural communities.

The project ran from January to December 2021 in New York, USA, and required deep cultural understanding to create messaging that resonated across different Latino subcultures while maintaining brand consistency and premium positioning.

## Key Achievements

- **Led multicultural marketing launch** resulting in **300% increase in social media engagement**
- **Executed targeted paid media campaigns** across Meta, TikTok, YouTube, and Spotify platforms
- **Enhanced reach within diverse audiences**, boosting click-through rates by **45%**
- **Utilized social listening tools and trend forecasting** to optimize messaging, driving **25% uplift in consumer sentiment**
- Developed bilingual creative assets that maintained cultural authenticity across platforms
- Established influencer partnerships that amplified brand reach within target demographics

## Philosophy

Authentic multicultural marketing requires deep cultural understanding, not just translation. Success comes from genuinely connecting with the values, celebrations, and lived experiences of diverse communities.

## Technologies & Methodologies Used

- **Multicultural Marketing** frameworks
- **Influencer Partnership** strategies
- **Paid Media** platforms (Meta, TikTok, YouTube, Spotify)
- **Social Listening** tools
- **Trend Forecasting** analytics
- **Bilingual Content** development
- **Consumer Sentiment** analysis

## Challenges and Learnings

The primary challenge was creating messaging that resonated authentically across multiple Latino subcultures without resorting to stereotypes.  This required extensive cultural research, community engagement, and iterative testing with diverse focus groups.

## Outcome

The campaign successfully launched Buchanan's Piña with unprecedented engagement levels, establishing the product as culturally relevant and premium within target demographics.
