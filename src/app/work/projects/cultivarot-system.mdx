## Overview

Developed **Cultivárot**—a revolutionary decision-making system focused on interconnected systems thinking and personal development.  The platform bridges analytical frameworks with intuitive guidance.

## Philosophy

**"Cultivating strategic awareness through systematic analytical frameworks for comprehensive personal and professional development."**

The name "Cultivárot" combines "cultivate" (intentional growth) with "tarot" (symbolic decision-making).

## Key Achievements

- Created **innovative decision-making system** integrating systems thinking
- Developed **unique analytical interpretations** for strategic planning
- Built **engaged community** of strategic thinkers
- Established **new paradigm for strategic planning** honoring analytical rigor and holistic thinking
- Successfully **merged analytical precision with intuitive guidance**

## System Components

1. **Interconnected Analysis**: Understanding how decisions ripple across life domains
2. **Temporal Perspective**: Considering short, medium, and long-term implications
3. **Values Alignment**: Ensuring decisions reflect core values
4. **Pattern Recognition**: Identifying recurring themes
5. **Strategic Frameworks**: Structured approaches to complex choices
6. **Intuitive Integration**: Honoring gut feelings alongside analysis

## Technologies & Methodologies Used

- **Systems Thinking Analysis**
- **Strategic Decision Frameworks**
- **Personal Development Strategy**
- **Interconnected Analytics**
- Consciousness expansion methodologies
- Holistic thinking approaches

## Outcome

Cultivárot users report making more confident, holistic decisions that consider multiple dimensions of impact.
